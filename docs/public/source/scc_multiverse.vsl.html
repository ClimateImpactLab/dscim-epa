scc_multiverse.vsl.calculate_vsl.calculate_vsl(ssp, data_path)[source]¶

Calculates value of statistical life under various assumptions.

Calculates VSLs for six valuation scenarios across two dimensions.
  • Three assumptions regarding VSL values include:
    • Value of statistical life (VSL), where each death is valued

      equally,

    • Value of life years (VLY), life years are valued equally,

    • Murphy Topel, heterogeneous valuation of life years.

  • Two assumptions regarding income scaling include:

    • Values directly scaled based upon the ratio of GDP to US GDP

    (on which the initial EPA VSL value is based.) - Population-weighted average of the scaled values above.

See Carleton et al. (2019) for a more detailed discussion of these assumptions.

Parameters

  • ssp (SSP scenario for which to calculate VSLs.) –

  • data_path (location of mortality repository data folder.) –
