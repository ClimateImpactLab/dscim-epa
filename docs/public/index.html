SCC Multiverse: A pythoon librrarry to calculate CIL’s Social Cost of Carbon¶

scc_multiverse is an open-sourced project developed at the Climate Impact Lab at the University of Chicago. This library will read results from different monetized damage projections from different realms (also knows as sectors, in CIL parlance) and allow the user to diagnose, aggregate, and calculate the social cost of carbon: the social welfare cost of an additional metric ton of CO2 in monetary terms.

The scc_multiverse offers an easy framework to load and quickly analyze data. Most of the code relies heavily on xarray and Dask. The first is essential to do different operations based on labels, while the second one allows bigger-than-memory calculations, while offering a generalizable interface that can be executed on a local machine, and HPC system, or a cloud computing service.
